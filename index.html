Our Mission

At the Church of Pentecost, our mission is to spread the gospel of Jesus Christ and make disciples of all nations. We believe that through faith in Christ, people can find salvation, healing, and wholeness in all areas of life.

Through our various ministries and outreach programs, we seek to share the love of Christ with those in need and provide opportunities for growth and spiritual development.

Our Beliefs

The Church of Pentecost is a Bible-based church that believes in the teachings of the Holy Scriptures. We believe in one God, who exists in three persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

We believe that salvation is a free gift from God, received by grace through faith in Jesus Christ. We also believe in the power of prayer, the importance of fellowship with other believers, and the need to live a holy and righteous life according to the teachings of Christ.

Join Us

If you're looking for a community of believers who love and serve God, we invite you to join us at the Church of Pentecost. We offer a variety of services and programs for people of all ages and backgrounds, and we would love to welcome you into our family.
