Upcoming Events

Here are some upcoming events at The Church of Pentecost:

Women's Conference

Date: May 1st, 2023

Time: 9:00am - 3:00pm

Location: The Church of Pentecost, Osu, Accra

The annual Women's Conference is a time of worship, learning, and fellowship for women of all ages. Join us as we hear from inspiring speakers, engage in small group discussions, and connect with other women in our community.

Youth Rally

Date: June 15th, 2023

Time: 2:00pm - 6:00pm

Location: The Church of Pentecost, Adum, Kumasi

Join us for an afternoon of worship and fun with other young people in the church. The Youth Rally will feature music, games, and a message from our youth pastor on how to live out our faith in today's world.

Prayer and Fasting

Date: July 30th - August 1st, 2023

Time: All day

Location: The Church of Pentecost, Tema Community 9

Join us for a weekend of prayer and fasting as we seek God's guidance and direction for our lives and our church. The weekend will include times of corporate prayer, individual reflection, and a special service on Sunday morning to break the fast together.
